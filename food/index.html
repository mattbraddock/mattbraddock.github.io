Granola
  • Ingredients
    • 3 1/2 cups old fashioned oats
    • 1 cup sunflower kernels
    • 1/2 cup slivered almonds
    • 1/2 cup walnuts, roughly chopped
    • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
    • 1 tsp ground ginger
    • 1/2 cup maple syrup
    • 1/2 cup grapeseed oil OR safflower oil
  • Directions
    • Preheat oven to 350F
    • Combine oats, nuts, seeds, and spices
    • Separately mix oil and syrup, then combine and coat
    • Bake in a greased glass dish for 30 minutes, stirring once halfway through
Spiced Nuts
  • Ingredients
    • 1/2 tsp cumin
    • 1/2 tsp paprika
    • 1/2 tsp habanero salt
    • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
    • 1/4 tsp red curry powder
    • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
    • 1/4 tsp turmeric
    • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
    • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
    • 1/4 tsp cardamom
    • 2 tbs olive oil
    • 1 cup raw almonds or cashews
    • 1 cup raw walnut halves and pieces
  • Directions
    • Preheat oven to 325F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or silicone baking mat
    • Combine all spices, then add oil and stir until well combined
    • Add nuts and stir until evenly coated
    • Spread nuts on the baking sheet and bake for 13-15 minutes, stirring once halfway through
    • Once removed from the oven, stir, let cool for an hour, then store in an airtight container
Barbecue Sauce
  • Ingredients (ketchup)
    • 14 oz can crushed tomatoes
    • 1/3 cup maple syrup
    • 3 tbs white vinegar
    • 2 tbs apple cider vinegar
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/4 tsp onion powder
    • 1/8 tsp garlic powder
    • 1/16 tsp allspice
  • Ingredients (barbecue sauce)
    • 1 cup ketchup (above)
    • 1 tsp canola oil
    • 1/3 cup light brown sugar
    • 2 tbs blackstrap molasses
    • 2 tsp smoked paprika
    • 1 tsp red chili powder
    • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
    • 1 tsp onion powder
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1/4 tsp ground mustard
  • Directions
    • Use a food processor to puree the crushed tomatoes
    • Combine the tomatoes and remaining ketchup ingredients in a saucepan and cook on medium heat until the consistency thickens slightly, stirring frequently
    • Combine the barbesauce ingredients with 1 cup of the ketchup in a saucepan and cook until desired consistency, stirring frequently
Soft Pretzels
  • Ingredients (dough)
    • 250 grams & 50 grams all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp granulated sugar
    • 1 cup warm water
    • 2 1/4 tsp instant yeast
  • Ingredients (bath & wash)
    • 10 cups & 1 tbs water
    • 2/3 cup baking soda
    • 1 egg yolk
    • coarse kosher salt
  • Directions
    • Mix together 250 grams of flour, salt, and sugar
    • Add warm water and mix to combine, then let sit for 15 minutes
    • Add yeast and mix, then transfer to a lightly floured surface and knead using the remaining 50 grams of flour
    • Transfer dough to a greased bowl and cover to rest for 30 minutes
    • In a shallow but wide saucepan, boil 10 cups of water with baking soda
    • Preheat oven to 450F
    • Transfer dough to a lightly greased surface, press the dough down, then divide it evenly into 8 pieces and let rest for 5 minutes
    • For each piece, roll the dough into a rope between 2 and 2.5 feet long, shape into a pretzel, and place in the boiling water for 30 seconds; transfer pretzels to a paper towel once removed
    • Combine the egg yolk and 1 tbs water to make the egg wash, move the pretzels to a baking sheet, and apply the egg wash and sprinkle kosher salt on the pretzels
    • Bake the pretzels for 10 minutes, then transfer to a drying rack to cool
